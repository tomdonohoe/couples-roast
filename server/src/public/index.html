Here are the rules:

  • Couples Roast is for 3-8 players.
  • The game consists of 4 rounds.
  • Each round every player is sent the same photo related to the category of that round
  • The player must come up with a great burn on another couple
  • Following the round everyone votes for the best burn!
  • You get 100 points for each vote you recieved.
  • The player with most votes gets a bonus 200 points.
  • The player with the most points at the end of four rounds wins.
